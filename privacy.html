Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Inkwake collects through this website, how it's used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form: name, email, project category, and budget range.
  • Careers application form: name, phone number, email, role applied for, cover letter, and resume file (PDF).
  • Automatically: basic technical data (browser type, device, pages visited) if analytics tools are enabled.

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to project enquiries and prepare proposals.
  • To review job applications and contact candidates.
  • To improve the website's content and performance.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Form Processing

Messages sent through the Contact and Careers application forms on this site are processed by Web3Forms, a third-party form-delivery service, which forwards submissions — including any resume file you upload — directly to Inkwake's email inbox. Web3Forms processes this data solely to deliver it to us and does not use it for any other purpose. By submitting a form, you acknowledge that your name, contact details, message content, and any attached file (such as a resume) will pass through this third-party service before reaching Inkwake.

Messages sent via WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or Instagram are subject to those platforms' own privacy policies, in addition to this one.

4. Data Retention

Contact and application data is retained only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry or complete a hiring process, unless you ask us to delete it sooner.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing email@inkwake.com.

6. Cookies

This site shows a cookie consent banner on first visit. Accepting or declining is stored locally in your browser only — no tracking cookies are set, and no data is shared with advertisers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out at email@inkwake.com.
